About Me

I was born in Noapara, Jashore — a beautiful town in my lovely country, Bangladesh. I was the youngest child 👶 in my family, and being the ‘baby’ of the house, I was always surrounded by love 💖 and laughter 😄, which made my childhood filled with warmth 🌞 and joy 🥰.

Later, my family moved to proper Jashore, a place rich in culture and heritage 🏰. It was there, amidst the vibrant streets and the echoes of history, that I spent my teenage years 🌱. It was in Jashore that I completed my secondary and higher schooling 🎓, and those years became a foundation for the person I am today.


All my life, I dreamt of becoming an engineer 👩‍🔧. As a child, I used to watch TV commercials where engineers stood confidently on scenic sites, wearing safety helmets under the open sky ⛅. I was mesmerized by that world and dreamt of becoming someone like that—building something meaningful, leaving a mark on the world ✨.


But life has its own way of weaving stories 🌸. In 2017, I got admitted to Khulna University, Bangladesh 🇧🇩, earning the 46th position in the entrance exam. Instead of civil engineering, fate led me into the fascinating world of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) 💻—a path I hadn't imagined, but one that became a turning point in my journey 🚀.


But the path was far from easy 🌪️. CSE at Khulna University broke me, tested me, and reshaped me. I stumbled through fear—of programming, of retakes—but I rose again, stronger each time 🌱. Over time, I fell in love with coding 💻, embraced challenges, and turned struggles into stories of growth. What began as fear transformed into passion, and I completed the journey with pride. KU will always be my second home 🏡—a place that broke me just enough to build me back, better.


I feel incredibly lucky to be part of the fascinating world of Computer Science 💻. To me, CS is more than just technology—it's a powerful tool for solving diverse problems across our society and the world at large 🌍. I’m especially drawn to research that creates real impact, from everyday individuals to academic communities 📚.


My passion lies at the intersection of Software Engineering and AI-driven automation, where I strive to bridge the gap between these domains. Alongside this, my current focus revolves around data analysis, data engineering, and data science 📊. Lately, I’ve been deeply intrigued by the transformative potential of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI 🤖✨.


Beyond academics and research, I have a wide range of interests that bring color to my life 🎨. Flowers, books, chocolates, forests, and all things green 🍃📚🍫 reflect the essence of who I am. I find joy pouring my heart into creating a warm, nurturing home 🏡.


Currently, I’m living in Arlington with my husband, embracing this beautiful phase of life as we grow and explore together 🌿. If all goes well, I’m truly excited to begin my next academic chapter—pursuing a Master’s in Information Systems at UTA 🤍—a journey I look forward to with hope and determination.


And that’s me—a dreamer, a doer, and a lifelong learner, always striving to grow, give, and go beyond. ✨
